About Kirwara Vocational Training Center

Kirwara Vocational Training Center (KVTC) is a registered vocational institution dedicated to equipping learners with practical, hands-on skills that meet current industry demands. We focus on empowering youth and adults with technical knowledge that leads to employment, self-reliance, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality technical and vocational training that empowers learners with relevant skills for the job market and self-employment.

Our Vision

To be a leading vocational training center in Kenya, producing skilled, disciplined, and innovative professionals.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity and professionalism
  • Practical skills and innovation
  • Discipline and teamwork
  • Community empowerment

Our Training Programs

We offer market-driven courses designed to meet local and national industry needs. Our programs combine theory with practical training.

  • Information Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Electrical Installation
  • Motor Vehicle Mechanics
  • Building Construction (Floor tilling and Painting)
  • Hairdressing & Beauty Therapy
  • Fashion Design & Tailoring
  • Catering
